# Privacy Policy / Chính sách bảo mật
_Last updated: 2025-12-02_

Welcome to **Album Gái Xinh** ("we", "our", or "us").  
We operate the website **https://albumgaixinh.io.vn** and provide a Facebook application that allows users to view and share photo collections.

## 1. Information We Collect
- Public profile information from Facebook (name, profile picture, user ID).
- Email address (if user grants permission).
- Interaction data such as likes, comments, and shares.
- Technical information such as IP, browser, device type.

## 2. How We Use Information
- To personalize user experience.
- To publish posts on behalf of users when permission is granted.
- To provide Facebook Auto-Post functionality.
- To improve our service quality and prevent system abuse.
- To respond to support requests via email.

We **do not sell, share or disclose** your information to any third party.

## 3. Facebook Permissions
Our application may request:
- `pages_manage_posts`
- `pages_show_list`
- `pages_read_engagement`
- `pages_read_user_content`
- `public_profile`
- `email`

These permissions are **used only to publish posts** on user’s Facebook Page and to improve the auto-posting service.

## 4. Data Retention
We store only essential log information for system operation.  
Users may request full deletion at any time.

## 5. Data Deletion Requests
If you want to delete your Facebook data from our system, send an email to:
support@albumgaixinh.io.vn
Title: `Data Deletion Request`

We will handle the request within **48 hours**.

## 6. Contact
For any privacy-related questions:
- Email: **support@albumgaixinh.io.vn**
- Website: https://albumgaixinh.io.vn
